About METAR

METAR (Meteorological Aerodrome Report) is a standardized format for reporting weather information at airports worldwide. This tool fetches the latest METAR data and translates it into plain English using metric units.

Examples of airport codes:

  • KJFK - New York JFK
  • EGLL - London Heathrow
  • YSSY - Sydney Airport
  • EDDF - Frankfurt Airport
  • RJTT - Tokyo Haneda
